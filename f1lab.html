LABORATORIES

Where innovation meets practical learning

Machine Learning Lab

In this lab, students work on Network programs and microprogramming. It contains 32 systems with multimedia facilities and software such as Keil IDE for 8051, Packet Tracer, etc. The lab also includes 28 microprocessor kits (8085).

Network Lab

A specialized facility equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure dedicated to the study and practical implementation of computer networks. It provides a hands-on environment for students to strengthen networking skills and gain real-world experience.
