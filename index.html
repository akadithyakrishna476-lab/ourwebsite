About the Department

The Information Technology Department at Government Engineering College, Idukki (GECI), established in 2000 alongside the college, is one of its core engineering branches under the Directorate of Technical Education, Government of Kerala. The department is supported by highly qualified and dedicated faculty who promote both academic excellence and holistic student development. Offering a balanced curriculum that blends theoretical foundations, hands-on laboratory work, and exposure to emerging technologies, the department is equipped with modern computer labs and smart classrooms . It also offers M.Tech programs in specialized areas such as Network Engineering, with admissions based on GATE scores. Facilities include a digitally managed library, advanced laboratories, workshops, and student support cells—all set within a lush, serene campus that fosters learning and innovation.

Vision

“To be a centre of excellence in the field of InformationTechnology which nurtures young minds through technological adaptation, research, innovation and entrepreneurial skills complying to the needs of society and industry.”


Mission

  • Imparting career oriented professional education to students through effective delivery of the curriculum.
  • Establishing effective adaptation of cutting edge technologies through industry collaborations and mentoring.
  • Empowering individuals to be socially responsible by inculcating critical thinking, innovation and ethical values.
