The M.Tech Computer Network Engineering program is designed to prepare engineers and technical professionals for the specialized domain of network engineering. This program nurtures graduates who can adapt to the dynamic needs of industries and organizations, while maintaining high professional and ethical values.

Students build a strong foundation in Information Technology, electronics, and data communication, along with advanced expertise in network engineering, distributed computing, and communication systems. Through a blend of theory and hands-on learning, they develop the ability to design, install, manage, and secure computer networks across sectors including industry, commerce, and the internet.

With access to modern laboratories, students gain practical exposure to creating real-world network solutions using the latest technologies. This holistic training opens pathways to careers in wireless and ad-hoc networks, protocol engineering, network design, and emerging IT domains.
