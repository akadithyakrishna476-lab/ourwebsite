Technical Events

C-Bootcamp

The Department of Information Technology organized a 5-Day C Bootcamp from 29th April to 3rd May at the ML Lab, IT/CS Block. The sessions were handled by Prof. Ratheesh T K, Assistant Professor, IT Department. Students gained hands-on experience in C programming, strengthening their coding fundamentals.

Figma Workshop

On 1st August, the IEEE Computer Society SBC GECI, in collaboration with the IT Association, organized a hands-on Figma Workshop at the Machine Learning Lab, IT/CS Block. The session was led by Biswas Sekhar and introduced students to UI/UX design fundamentals, prototyping, and collaborative workflows.

GameCraft: Build Your First Game

The µLearn IDK community conducted a workshop titled “GameCraft: Build Your First Game” on 18th August at the IT Lab. The session was handled by Nithin N (S5 CSE) and Sanju Sreejith (S5 IT). Participants learned the basics of game development and created their first playable game.
